                        DELETE PROPOSAL - HOWTO
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Only user, who are owners of a proposal, have the rights to delete it.
   
2.  In the repository browser, open the proposal to delete. A 'Delete' button will
    then be shown in the proposal viewer screen. 

3.  On click of this 'Delete' button, a confirmation is asked from user.  
    
4.  On click of 'Delete' button on confirmation dialogue, the proposal will be 
    permanently deleted from the repository.
